Elmbridge Manor

Elmbridge Manor is a large, up-scale and high priced wedding venue sought out by David Rose and Patrick Brewer during their engagement.

Managed by Clive, Elmbridge Manor is set up into three pricing packages, gold, silver, and bronze; they can accommodate almost any request, however they don't give discounted prices and don't provide pricing in advance. They do, however, offer a much reduced rate on the first Sunday of every month, as that's when a local farm down the road process their pork. This farm also provides catering for Elmbridge Manor; they have fresh cheeses, breads, and meats.
