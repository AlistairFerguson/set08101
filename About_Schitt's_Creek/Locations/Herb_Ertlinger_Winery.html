Herb Ertlinger Winery

The Herb Ertlinger Winery is a small, local winery located in Schitt's Creek which specialises in fruit wines and is owned by vintner Herb Ertlinger. Actress Moira Rose served briefly as a spokesperson for the winery.

Moira was later used again to promote the winery’s new Moira Rosé after the release and success of the new Crows film.
