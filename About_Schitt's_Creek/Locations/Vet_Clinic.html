Vet Clinic

Schitt's Creek Veterinary Clinic is a veterinary operated by Ted Mullens which services the domesticated animals of Schitt's Creek.

After her community service, Alexis gained employment at the clinic, where she got to know Ted a lot more. This is the primary location in which their romance developed.

Along with the clinic, there is also an upstairs flat, which is where Ted lives.
