Schitt's Creek:

"Where Everyone Fits In"

"If heaven had a creek, it would be this one"

Schitt's Creek is a small settlement within the township of Elmdale in Ontario, Canada, founded in 1895 by Horace Schitt and his sister. The town is shown to be the home to largely unsophisticated, but honest, down-to-Earth, and hard working people who are always eager to give a helping hand, or a happy smile to anyone in need, especially the Rose Family.

While the town is small, there are various points of interest and numerous personalities.
