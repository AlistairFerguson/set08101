Bob Currie

Bob torments Johnny almost as much as Roland does. However, whenever Johnny needs help, Bob loves nothing more than to help him out as much as he can with a great big smile on his face; even if his help always ends up frustrating Johnny's plans. There's nothing Bob enjoys more than sharing a magazine, or the latest Rose misadventure, with his handsome wife, Gwen, whom Bob initially believes he's happily married to. And while they are indeed a united front early on, they have an acrimonious separation when Gwen leaves him for their minister and eventually takes Bob’s home too, promptly kicking him out.

Bob attempts to navigate single life following his split with Gwen and stays with Roland and Jocelyn. He also gets taken out by Johnny, Roland, and Ronnie to meet women; He doesn't, however, find much luck. On David's wedding day, Bob offers his garage for parking — on the condition that a space remain clear "in case Gwen comes back;" Even with Bob being clearly separated from his wife, he still holds out hope that Gwen will return to him.
