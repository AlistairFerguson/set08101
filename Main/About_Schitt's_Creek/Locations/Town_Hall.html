Town Hall

The Schitt's Creek Town Hall is the base of operations for the local government in Schitt's Creek, incorporated in 1895. It is here where the Mayor and Town Council offices are located and where town meetings take place.

Other events such as Asbestos Fest, the Schitt's Creek Flea, and David Rose and Patrick Brewer's wedding were also held in the town hall.

Rehearsals for the all-female a Capella group, the Jazzagals also takes place inside the town hall, as well as rehearsals for Cabaret, the musical production.
