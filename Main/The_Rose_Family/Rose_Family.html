The Rose Family

The main characters of the series, the Rose family are a little disfunctional. Upon their arrival to Schitt's Creek, they don't seem to know much about eachother. As they adjust to their new less sophisticated lives in Schitt's Creek, they grow a lot closer and become an important part of eachother's lives
